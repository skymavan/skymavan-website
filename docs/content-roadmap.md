# SkyMavan Content Roadmap

The launch site is intentionally one page. Future content should expand topical authority only when SkyMavan can publish useful, experience-backed material rather than thin keyword pages.

## Pillar 1: AI agents in business operations

- **What is a business AI agent?** — searchable awareness guide defining agents, tools, memory, approvals, and suitable workflows.
- **AI agents vs workflow automation** — searchable consideration article comparing when reasoning is useful and when deterministic automation is safer.

## Pillar 2: AI workflow automation and human approvals

- **How to automate an approval workflow with AI** — searchable implementation guide with a concrete human-in-the-loop architecture.
- **How much does custom AI development cost?** — searchable decision guide explaining the cost drivers behind agents, integrations, and production hardening.

## Pillar 3: Building and operating AI SaaS products

- **Build vs buy an AI SaaS product** — searchable consideration guide for founders deciding between vendors, internal teams, and custom product development.
- **RAG application development guide** — searchable technical guide covering retrieval quality, evaluation, permissions, observability, and operating cost.

Every article should include a named author, visible update date, direct answer introduction, original diagrams or examples, cited primary sources, and links back to the relevant service and pricing section.

## Post-launch search setup

After the production domain is live:

1. Verify `https://skymavan.com/` in Google Search Console and Bing Webmaster Tools.
2. Submit `https://skymavan.com/sitemap.xml` to both services.
3. Record impressions, average position, clicks, and AI-answer citations monthly for the commercial query set below.
